About Us

The idea for this project was initially proposed by our friend, Parth Shadriya, who envisioned a music player that provides downloadable online music without any advertisements. While he successfully implemented this concept on a website, we saw an opportunity to bring the same experience to Android users—making it even more accessible and convenient.

However, managing both online and offline music playback on Android posed several technical challenges. To tackle this, we decided to split the app into two components: Zolt, which handles the online music experience, and Pookie Zolt, which operates offline. This separation allows us to offer a smoother and more reliable user experience.

We are both diploma students in the computer field, passionate about learning and building creative digital solutions. Despite the long hours and occasional frustration that come with screen time and debugging, we stayed committed—especially when motivated by how disruptive and repetitive ads can be on major platforms like Spotify. Our solution provides an easy and enjoyable alternative.

We hope you appreciate the effort behind this app and enjoy using it as much as we enjoyed building it.
